Business Intelligence Specialist Job Description

Position: Business Intelligence Specialist
Department: Data Analytics
Location: New York, NY
Experience Level: 5-8 years

JOB SUMMARY
We are seeking an experienced Business Intelligence Specialist to join our growing data analytics team. The ideal candidate will have strong expertise in BI tools, data analysis, and reporting solutions to support data-driven decision making across the organization.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
• Design and develop comprehensive BI dashboards and reports using Power BI and Tableau
• Create complex SQL queries and stored procedures for data extraction and analysis
• Build automated reporting systems to streamline business processes
• Collaborate with stakeholders to define KPIs and business metrics
• Develop and maintain data models and dimensional schemas
• Perform data analysis to identify trends and business opportunities
• Manage ETL processes and data pipeline optimization
• Create documentation for BI processes and data governance
• Train end users on self-service analytics and BI tools
• Ensure data quality and integrity across reporting systems

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
• Bachelor's degree in Data Analytics, Computer Science, or related field
• 5-8 years of experience in business intelligence and data analytics
• Expert knowledge of BI tools (Power BI, Tableau, QlikView)
• Advanced SQL skills and database management experience
• Experience with data warehousing concepts and methodologies
• Proficiency in statistical analysis and data visualization
• Knowledge of ETL tools and processes (SSIS, Azure Data Factory)
• Experience with cloud platforms (Azure, AWS, GCP)
• Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
• Excellent communication and stakeholder management abilities

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
• Master's degree in Data Analytics or related field
• Microsoft Power BI certification
• Tableau certification
• Experience with Python or R for data analysis
• Knowledge of machine learning and predictive analytics
• Experience with Agile development methodologies

TECHNICAL SKILLS
• Business Intelligence: Power BI, Tableau, QlikView, Looker
• Databases: SQL Server, Oracle, MySQL, PostgreSQL
• Programming: SQL, Python, R, DAX, MDX
• Cloud: Azure, AWS, Google Cloud Platform
• ETL: SSIS, Azure Data Factory, Informatica
• Reporting: SSRS, automated reporting solutions
