SharePoint Developer Job Description

Position: SharePoint Developer
Department: IT Development
Location: San Francisco, CA
Experience Level: 5-7 years

JOB SUMMARY
We are looking for a skilled SharePoint Developer to design, develop, and maintain SharePoint solutions for our organization. The successful candidate will have extensive experience with SharePoint development, customization, and the Microsoft 365 ecosystem.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
• Develop custom SharePoint solutions using SPFx, React, and TypeScript
• Design and implement SharePoint Online sites and workflows
• Create Power Apps and Power Automate solutions for business processes
• Develop custom web parts and SharePoint Framework applications
• Migrate legacy SharePoint solutions to modern SharePoint Online
• Implement SharePoint governance policies and best practices
• Integrate SharePoint with Microsoft Teams and other M365 services
• Provide SharePoint administration and configuration support
• Create custom forms, lists, and content types
• Develop and maintain documentation for SharePoint solutions

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
• Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
• 5-7 years of SharePoint development experience
• Expertise in SharePoint Online and SharePoint Server
• Proficiency in SPFx, React, TypeScript, and JavaScript
• Experience with C#, .NET Framework, and ASP.NET
• Knowledge of Power Platform (Power Apps, Power Automate, Power BI)
• Experience with Microsoft 365 services integration
• Understanding of SharePoint architecture and best practices
• Experience with REST APIs and web services
• Strong problem-solving and analytical skills

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
• Microsoft 365 Developer certification
• SharePoint Developer certification
• Experience with Azure development
• Knowledge of modern web development frameworks
• Experience with DevOps and CI/CD pipelines

TECHNICAL SKILLS
• SharePoint: SharePoint Online, SharePoint Server, SPFx
• Frontend: React, TypeScript, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3
• Backend: C#, .NET Framework, .NET Core, ASP.NET
• Power Platform: Power Apps, Power Automate, Power BI
• Microsoft 365: Teams, OneDrive, Exchange Online
• Development Tools: Visual Studio, VS Code, PowerShell
• Version Control: Git, Azure DevOps
• APIs: REST, Graph API, SharePoint APIs
